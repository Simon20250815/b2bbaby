## Rate Limits

- Maximum: 10 requests per minute per crawler
- Recommended delay: 5 seconds between requests
- Crawl-delay: 5 (configured in robots.txt)

## Crawling Best Practices

1. Respect robots.txt directives
2. Use proper User-Agent headers
3. Implement crawl delays as specified
4. Avoid aggressive crawling patterns
5. Follow hreflang annotations for language versions
